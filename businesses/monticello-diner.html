💬 About Monticello Diner

The Monticello Diner is the kind of place that defines small-town Georgia dining. Open early every morning and busy from the first coffee, this local institution has been feeding Jasper County residents for years with honest, scratch-cooked Southern food. The breakfast menu reads like a greatest hits of Georgia comfort cooking — country ham, biscuits and gravy, eggs any style, grits, and pancakes that people drive from neighboring counties to eat. Lunch brings daily specials, sandwiches, and plate lunches served fast and generous. The staff knows regulars by name, newcomers are welcomed warmly, and the coffee is always hot. If you want to understand what Monticello is about, eat one meal here.
