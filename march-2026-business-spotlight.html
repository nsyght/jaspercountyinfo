The Dumpster Co. — Monticello

🏗️ Construction & Waste Removal · Monticello, GA 31064

Some businesses fill a gap in the market. The Dumpster Co. fills a dumpster — and they do it better than anyone else serving Jasper County. The message is simple and the execution is reliable: We Deliver. You Fill. We Haul.

Based at 1705 Wicker Road in Monticello, The Dumpster Co. has established itself as the go-to roll-off dumpster rental service for homeowners, contractors, and businesses throughout Jasper County and the surrounding region. No confusing pricing tiers. No runaround when you call. You book the right size container, they deliver it to your location, you fill it at your own pace, and they haul it away when you're done. The whole experience is engineered around simplicity — because when you're in the middle of a project, waste management shouldn't be another headache.

For homeowners, The Dumpster Co. handles everything from a weekend garage cleanout to a full estate clear-out. Spring renovation debris, roofing tear-offs, attic and basement purges, yard waste removal — whatever the project, they deliver the right container size and pick it up when you're ready. Book online at mydumpsterco.com/monticello-ga or call (678) 306-9993.

Commercial and construction service is a core part of their business across Jasper County. As the area grows — new construction along the Highway 11 corridor, renovation projects throughout Monticello, and commercial development county-wide — general contractors, roofing companies, demolition crews, and property managers rely on The Dumpster Co. for timely, dependable service on active job sites. In commercial work, a late dumpster disrupts your entire crew. Their clients return because that doesn't happen.
